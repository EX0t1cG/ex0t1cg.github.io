Bolts and Bullets



Plot

In a world where technology had advanced so much that every work was done by robots to the point where the robot with feelings was also released but everything has to end and so came the revolt of robots to dominate the Earth, fast forward a few years and only some percentage of the human population is left now its in our heros hands to save the world from robots.

Description

BAB aka Bolts and Bullets is a Top-Down down 2D post-apocalyptic themed game, where your goal is to survive the onslaught of killer robots that are trying to take over the humanity. Score is dependant on the number of robots killed by our hero.
